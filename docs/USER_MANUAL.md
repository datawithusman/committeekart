## 1. CommitteeKart Kya Hai? (What is CommitteeKart?)

CommitteeKart ek digital tracker hai jo aapki committee (savings circle)
ko manage karta hai. Lekin ek zaroori baat:

> **CommitteeKart paisa hold nahi karta.**
> (CommitteeKart does NOT hold your money.)

Yeh sirf record rakhta hai:
- Kisne payment ki (who paid)
- Kitni amount thi (how much)
- Kab milli (when)
- Kis method se (cash, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, bank transfer)

Aapka paisa aapke paas rehta hai. CommitteeKart sirf transparency
deta hai taake koi disagreement na ho.

### Committee Kaise Kaam Karta Hai (How a Committee Works)

Ek committee mein kuch log (members) har mahine ek fixed amount
detay hain. Har mahine ek member ko "pot" milta hai (total amount).
Har member ko bari se ek baar pot milta hai.

**Example:**
- 10 members, har mahine Rs. 5,000
- Total pot har mahine = Rs. 50,000
- 10 mahine tak chalta hai
- Har member ko ek baar Rs. 50,000 milta hai

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 Account Banana (Creating an Account)

CommitteeKart use karne ke liye pehle account banana zaroori hai.

**Steps:**

1. CommitteeKart app ko open karein
2. "Sign up karein" par click karein

   [SCREENSHOT: Signup page with "Account banayein" heading and the signup form]

3. Apna **pura naam** daalein (e.g. "Ali Khan")
4. Apna **email** daalein (e.g. "ali@example.com")
5. **Password** daalein (kam az kam 6 characters)
6. "Account Banao" button par click karein

**Bas!** Aapka account ban gaya. Aap dashboard par redirect ho
jayenge.

> **Tip:** Password aisa rakhein jo yaad rakhna asaan ho lekin
> guess karna mushkil. Kam az kam 6 characters zaroori hai.

### 2.2 Login Karna (Logging In)

Agar aapke paas pehle se account hai:

1. CommitteeKart open karein
2. "Login" page par jayein

   [SCREENSHOT: Login page with "Wapas khush aamdeed!" heading]

3. Email daalein
4. Password daalein
5. "Login" button par click karein

Aap dashboard par aa jayenge.

### 2.3 Logout Karna (Logging Out)

Logout karne ke liye:

1. Dashboard par top right corner mein dekhein
2. "Logout" button par click karein

Aap wapas login page par aa jayenge.

> **Zaroori:** Agar shared computer use kar rahe hain, toh hamesha
> logout zaroor karein.

### 2.4 Dashboard Overview

Login karne ke baad aap dashboard par honge. Dashboard par aapko
yeh sab dikhega:

[SCREENSHOT: Dashboard showing welcome greeting, three stat cards (Total Committees, Total Collected, Pending Payments), and committee list]

- **Pending Payments** - kitni payments abhi pending hain

- **+ New Committee** button - nayi committee banane ke liye

## 3. For Organizers (Committee Chalane Wale)

Agar aap committee chalate hain (organizer hain), toh yeh section
aapke liye hai.

### 3.1 Nayi Committee Banana (Creating a New Committee)

1. Dashboard par **"+ New Committee"** button par click karein

   [SCREENSHOT: Create committee page with "Committee ki Details" section]

2. **Committee ka Naam** daalein
   - Example: "Office Committee", "Family Committee", "Flat Committee"
   - 1 se 100 characters

3. **Description** (optional) daalein
   - Committee ke baare mein kuch info
   - Example: "Har 1st tareekh payment deadline"

4. **Monthly Amount** daalein
   - Har member kitna dega har mahine
   - Example: 5000 (kam az kam Rs. 100 hona chahiye)

5. **Duration (Months)** daalein
   - **Zaroori:** Duration aur total members barabar hone chahiye
   - Agar 10 members hain toh 10 months
   - Agar 5 members hain toh 5 months
   - Kyun? Har member ko ek baar pot milna chahiye

6. **Draw Type** chunein:
   - **Lottery (Random):** System randomly decide karega kis month
     kisko pot milega
   - **Fixed Order:** Aap pehle se decide karenge kis month kisko
     milega
   - **Auction (Bidding):** Members bid karenge, highest bidder ko
     pot milega (monthly)

   > **Recommendation:** Agar unsure hain toh "Lottery" chunein.
   > Sabse fair aur popular hai.

7. **Start Date** daalein
   - Committee kab se shuru hogi

8. **Members Add Karein** (next section mein detail)

### 3.2 Members Add Karna (Adding Members)

Committee creation form mein aap members add karte hain.

[SCREENSHOT: Members section of create committee form with multiple name and phone input rows]

**Zaroori Jaano:**
- Aap khud (organizer) automatically pehle member ho
- Aap dusre members **naam aur phone** se add karte hain
- Members ka app par account hona zaroori nahi hai
- Kam az kam 1 aur member add karein (total kam az kam 2)

**Steps:**

1. Pehle member row mein naam daalein (e.g. "Ahmed Raza")
2. Phone daalein (optional, e.g. "03001234567")
3. "+ Add Member" par click karein aur agla member daalein
4. Jab saare members add ho jayein, count check karein:
   - Total members = aap + added members
   - Duration = total members

**Example:**
- Aap chahte hain 10 members ki committee
- Duration = 10 months
- Aapko 9 members add karne hain (aap 10th member ho)

> **Yaad Rakhein:** Duration aur total members barabar nahi honge
> toh error aayega. Yeh fairness ke liye hai: har member ko exactly
> ek baar pot milna chahiye.

### 3.3 Payments Track Karna (Tracking Payments)

Committee banane ke baad, aap har mahine payments track karte hain.

1. Committee detail page par jayein (dashboard se committee par click karein)

   [SCREENSHOT: Committee detail page with "Is Mahine ki Payments" section showing member rows with Paid/Pending buttons]

2. **"Is Mahine ki Payments"** section mein har member ka row hoga
3. Har row mein member ka naam, amount, aur ek button hai

**Mark Paid (Payment Ho Gai):**
- Jab koi member payment de de, uske row ka **"Pending"** button
  par click karein
- Button green ho jayega aur "Paid" likha aayega
- System automatically date aur time record kar leta hai

**Mark Pending (Wapas Pending):**
- Agar galti se paid mark kiya, ya payment wapas li, toh **"Paid"**
  button par dobara click karein
- Button wapas amber (pending) ho jayega

> **Note:** CommitteeKart paisa receive nahi karta. Aap paisa
> khud collect karte hain (cash, JazzCash, bank) aur yahan sirf
> record karte hain ke payment ho gai.

### 3.4 Draw Schedule Dekhna (Viewing the Draw Schedule)

Draw schedule batata hai kis month kisko pot milega.

1. Committee detail page par scroll karein
2. **"Draw Schedule"** section mein dekhein

[SCREENSHOT: Draw Schedule section showing months M1, M2, M3 with member names, dates, and pot amounts]

Har entry dikhayegi:
- **Month number** (M1, M2, M3, ...)
- **Member ka naam** jisko pot milega
- **Date** kab milega
- **Amount** kitni hogi (pot = monthly amount x members)

**Example (Lottery draw, 5 members, Rs. 5,000/month):**
- M1: Fatima Khan, 15 Jul 2026, Rs. 25,000
- M2: Ali Ahmed, 15 Aug 2026, Rs. 25,000
- M3: Sana Malik, 15 Sep 2026, Rs. 25,000
- M4: Bilal Raza, 15 Oct 2026, Rs. 25,000
- M5: Hina Ali, 15 Nov 2026, Rs. 25,000

### 3.5 Pot Kis Month Kisko Milega (Who Gets the Pot When)

Yeh janne ke liye:

1. Draw Schedule section dekhein (upar wala step)
2. Ya phir **"Saare Members"** section mein har member ke saamne
   "Pot: Month X" likha hai

[SCREENSHOT: Members list showing each member with their pot month]

**Example:**
- Fatima Khan: Pot: Month 1
- Ali Ahmed: Pot: Month 2

Iska matlab Fatima ko pehle mahine pot milega, Ali ko doosre mahine.

### 3.6 Committee Detail Page ke Sections

Committee detail page par 4 sections hote hain:

[SCREENSHOT: Full committee detail page with all four sections visible]

1. **Committee Header:** Naam, description, status, monthly amount,
   total pot, members, duration
2. **Is Mahine ki Payments:** Current month ke payments aur toggle
   buttons
3. **Draw Schedule:** Kaun kab pot lega
4. **Saare Members:** Saari members ki list unke pot month ke saath

## 5. FAQ - Aksar Poochhe Jane Wale Sawal

### Q1: Kya CommitteeKart paisa hold karta hai?

**Nahi.** CommitteeKart kabhi bhi aapka paisa hold, receive, ya
transfer nahi karta. Yeh sirf ek digital register hai jo record
rakhta hai. Paisa aap khud collect aur distribute karte hain.

### Q2: Kya yeh app free hai?

Haan, basic features free hain. Aane waale versions mein paid plans
(pro/premium) ho sakte hain with extra features, lekin basic committee
tracking hamesha available rahega.

### Q3: Members ka account banana zaroori hai?

Nahi. Organizer members ko sirf naam aur phone se add kar sakta hai.
Members ka app par account hona zaroori nahi. Yeh feature specially
un logon ke liye hai jo tech-savvy nahi hain (jaise older relatives).

### Q4: Agar galti se "Paid" mark kar diya toh?

Koi masla nahi. Dobara us button par click karein, woh wapas "Pending"
ho jayega. Aap kabhi bhi status change kar sakte hain.

### Q5: Duration aur members barabar kyun hone chahiye?

ROSCA (committee) ke rule ke hisaab se, har member ko exactly ek baar
pot milna chahiye. Agar 10 members hain aur 10 months hain, toh har
member ko ek baar pot milega. Agar yeh barabar nahi honge toh kisi ko
dobar pot milega ya kisi ko nahi milega, jo unfair hai.

### Q6: Lottery draw kaise kaam karta hai?

Jab aap committee banate hain aur "Lottery" select karte hain, toh
system saare members ko randomly shuffle kar deta hai. Har member ko
ek random month assign ho jata hai. Yeh fair hai kyunki koi bhi
member ko pehle se pata nahi hota.

### Q7: Fixed draw mein order kaise set karein?

Fixed draw mein members unke addition ke order mein assign hote hain.
Pehla add kiya gaya member pehle month mein pot lega, doosra doosre
month mein, etc.

### Q8: Kya main committee edit kar sakta hoon creation ke baad?

Abhi ke version mein, committee creation ke baad editing limited hai.
Aap contributions ka status change kar sakte hain. Committee ke main
settings (amount, duration, draw type) edit karna aane wale versions
mein ho ga.

### Q9: Agar kisi member ne payment nahi di toh?

Uska contribution "Pending" hi rehta hai. CommitteeKart automatically
"Late" mark nahi karta (abhi). Aap manually track kar sakte hain.
Aane wale versions mein automatic reminders aur late marking ho ga.

### Q10: Kya data secure hai?

Haan. Har user sirf apna data dekh sakta hai. Supabase ke Row Level
Security (RLS) policies ensure karte hain ke koi doosra user aapki
committees, members, ya contributions nahi dekh sakta.

### Q11: Mobile app kab aayega?

Abhi CommitteeKart ek web app hai jo mobile browser mein bhi chalta
hai. Native mobile app (Android aur iOS) aane wale versions mein
ho gi.

### Q12: Password bhool gaya toh?

Abhi ke version mein, password reset feature add hone wala hai.
Tab tak, agar password bhool gaye hain toh naya account bana lein
ya admin se contact karein.

### Q13: Kitne members tak ki committee bana sakte hain?

2 se 50 members tak. Minimum 2 (aap + 1 aur) aur maximum 50.

### Q14: Monthly amount kitni rakh sakte hain?

Kam az kam Rs. 100. Maximum limit nahi hai (database supports up to
very large amounts).

## 6. Troubleshooting (Masla Hal Karna)

### Masla 1: Login nahi ho raha (Cannot Log In)

**Symptoms:** "Email ya password ghalat hai" error aa raha hai.

**Solutions:**
1. Email aur password double check karein (capital/small letters)
2. Agar naya account hai, pehle signup karein
3. Agar password yaad nahi, naya account banayein (password reset
   feature coming soon)
4. Browser cookies clear karein aur dobara koshish karein

### Masla 2: Signup mein "Yeh email pehle se registered hai"

**Symptoms:** Email already exists error.

**Solutions:**
1. Iska matlab aapke paas pehle se account hai is email se
2. Login page par jayein aur login karein
3. Agar password yaad nahi toh alag email se naya account banayein

### Masla 3: "Duration aur members barabar hone chahiye" error

**Symptoms:** Committee creation mein error aa raha hai.

**Solutions:**
1. Total members count karein (aap + added members)
2. Duration ko same set karein
3. Example: Agar aapne 5 members add kiye hain (aap 6th ho) toh
   duration 6 set karein
4. Member rows add/remove karke count adjust karein

### Masla 4: Committee creation ke baad page blank ya error

**Symptoms:** Create Committee click karne ke baad kuch nahi hota
ya error aata hai.

**Solutions:**
1. Saari fields sahi se bharin (naam, amount, duration, date)
2. Kam az kam 1 member add karein
3. Start date past mein na ho (agar possible)
4. Internet connection check karein
5. Page refresh karke dobara koshish karein

### Masla 5: Dashboard par kuch nahi dikhta (Empty Dashboard)

**Symptoms:** Dashboard par koi committee nahi hai.

**Solutions:**
1. Yeh normal hai agar aapne abhi tak koi committee nahi banayi
2. "+ New Committee" par click karke pehli committee banayein

### Masla 6: Payment button kaam nahi kar raha

**Symptoms:** "Pending" ya "Paid" button par click karne se kuch
nahi hota.

**Solutions:**
1. Thoda wait karein (loading ho sakta hai)
2. Page refresh karein
3. Internet connection check karein
4. Agar error message aa raha hai toh usse note karein

### Masla 7: Protected page par access nahi ho raha

**Symptoms:** Dashboard ya committee page kholtay hi login page
par redirect ho jate hain.

**Solutions:**
1. Aap logged out ho. Pehle login karein
2. Agar login ke baad bhi masla hai toh cookies clear karein
3. Incognito mode mein try karein

### Masla 8: Numbers galat dikha rahe hain (Wrong Stats)

**Symptoms:** Dashboard ya committee page par amounts galat lag
rahe hain.

**Solutions:**
1. Page refresh karein (data update ho jayega)
2. Total = saari paid contributions ka sum hona chahiye
3. Agar phir bhi galat hai toh organizer check karein ke saari
   payments sahi mark hui hain

### Masla 9: Mobile par app slow hai

**Solutions:**
1. Internet connection check karein
2. Browser cache clear karein
3. App ko home screen par add karein (PWA feature) for faster access

### Masla 10: Date galat dikha raha hai

**Symptoms:** Payment due date ya draw date galat hai.

**Solutions:**
1. Committee ka start date check karein
2. Dates start date se calculate hote hain (month 0 = start month,
   month 1 = next month, etc.)
3. Time zone ki wajah se 1 din ka difference ho sakta hai

## 7. Glossary (Terms ka Matlab)

| Term | Matlab (Meaning) |
|------|-------------------|
| **Committee** | Ek group jo monthly savings karta hai aur rotation mein pot distribute karta hai. Also called ROSCA, chit fund, or savings circle. |
| **Organizer** | Wo shakhs jo committee chalata hai, members add karta hai, payments track karta hai. |
| **Member** | Committee ka hissa jo monthly contribution deta hai aur ek baar pot receive karta hai. |
| **Pot** | Ek mahine ka total collected amount (monthly amount x member count). Jaise: 10 members x Rs. 5,000 = Rs. 50,000 ka pot. |
| **Contribution** | Ek member ka monthly payment. |
| **Draw** | Process jo decide karta hai kis month kisko pot milega. |
| **Draw Schedule** | Full plan: har month kaunsa member pot lega. |
| **Lottery Draw** | Random shuffle. Har member randomly assigned hota hai. |
| **Fixed Draw** | Organizer pehle se decide karta hai order. |
| **Auction Draw** | Members bid karte hain. Highest bidder ko pot milta hai. |
| **Paid** | Payment record ho gai hai. |
| **Pending** | Payment abhi baaki hai. |
| **ROSCA** | Rotating Savings and Credit Association. Committee ka formal naam. |
| **RLS** | Row Level Security. Database feature jo ensure karta hai ke har user sirf apna data dekhe. |
| **Track, Don't Hold** | CommitteeKart ka principle: paisa track karo, hold na karo. |
